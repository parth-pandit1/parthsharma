Our students actively engage in adventure camps, national jamborees, and service programs through the Bharat Scouts & Guides movement. They learn self-discipline, teamwork, survival skills, and values of brotherhood through field training, volunteering, and outdoor learning.

National Cadet Corps (NCC)
